About Us

Mapara A Jazz is a South African Amapiano duo that took the world by storm with their viral hit "John Vuli Gate". Hailing from Pretoria, the group consists of Man Malaya Wa Tshwenya and Leonard Sbechu Malatji.

Formed in 2019, the duo quickly rose to fame with their unique blend of Amapiano rhythms and jazz influences. Their breakthrough single "John Vuli Gate" became a global sensation in 2021, sparking dance challenges across social media platforms and earning millions of streams.

Known for their energetic performances and infectious beats, Mapara A Jazz has performed at major events across South Africa and continues to be one of the most sought-after acts in the Amapiano scene.

Featured Artists

Lenny Sbechu The Big Boss

Full Name: Leonard Sbechu Malatji

Born: 1989

Hometown: Pretoria, South Africa

Leonard was raised in Pretoria, where his passion for music began at an early age. He later teamed up with Mano Nephawe (also known as Man Malaya Wa Tshwenya) to form Mapara A Jazz.

Leonard Sbechu Malatji, through Mapara A Jazz, has played a pivotal role in popularizing South African dance music globally. Their music not only entertains but also celebrates township life and African rhythms, making them cultural ambassadors of a uniquely local yet globally resonant sound.

Man Malaya Wa Tshwenya

Full Name: Mano Nephawe

Born: 1992

Hometown: Pretoria, South Africa

Mano Nephawe has helped shape the sound of modern South African dance music. His contributions through Mapara A Jazz have brought township rhythms to global audiences, making him a key figure in the rise of Amapiano and Bacardi house.
